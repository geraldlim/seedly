We are two independent startup hamsters, from NUS (National University of Singapore) and we are grateful to have the support of NUS Enterprise and East Ventures to launch our product

How does Seedly earn?

Here at Seedly, we believe in value! At the bottom of the infographic, you probably will notice that we recommend specific and personalised deals to you. If you do click on those deals, we earn from referral fees from our partners

Does Seedly store any of my information?

No, we never store any data, please refer to our Security policy above

Why do I not have an account?

We want to ensure autonomy and bring to you the best value as a beta user, thus we have decided to be a “no-frills, supplier of valuable information and unbiased advice”

Is this the final product?

No this is the beta version of our main product which will have tweaks to it
